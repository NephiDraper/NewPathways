Recovery

Our outpatient drug and alcohol rehab program incorporates unique therapies that quickly address and subdue the need for self medication. This therapy regimen isn’t available at any other outpatient rehab facility in Salt Lake City.

Individual Therapy

Individual therapy (sometimes called “psychotherapy” or “counseling”) is a process through which clients work one-on-one with a trained therapist—in a safe, caring, and confidential environment—to explore their feelings, beliefs, or behaviors, work through challenging or influential memories, identify aspects of their lives that they would like to change, better understand themselves and others, set personal goals, and work toward desired change.

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

Outpatient rehab programs allow you to continue to live at home during drug or alcohol rehabilitation. Outpatient rehab tends to cost less than residential or inpatient treatment, and it may be more suitable for those unable to take extended absences from work or personal obligations.

Virtual Float Tank Therapy

Our Virtual Float Tank allows your mind and body to float without getting wet! This meditative capsule transports you into a state of deep relaxation which will allow your mind and body to rejuvenate.

Therapeutic Massages

We offer several types of massage that focus on different parts of the body or healing approaches including: Swedish Massages, Deep Tissue Massages, Sports Massages, and Trigger Point Massages.

Infrared Saunas

Unlike a traditional sauna, our infrared saunas don’t heat the air around you. Instead, they use infrared lamps (that use electromagnetic radiation) to warm your body directly.

Red Light Therapy

This therapy uses specific wavelengths of light to treat skin issues, such as wrinkles, scars, persistent wounds, among other conditions. Also known as Photobiomodulation (PBM), Low Level Light Therapy (LLLT), Biostimulation, or Photonic Simulation.
